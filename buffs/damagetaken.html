Damage Taken Reduced Lowers all damage dealt to the unit by the percentage specified in the ability/perk

If an ability which grants is used on a unit which already has either or , then the with the higher percentage will take priority; with counting as 100% damage reduction. If percentages are equal then the original will take priority
