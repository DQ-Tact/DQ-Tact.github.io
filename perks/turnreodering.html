What are the Lives of the Meek, But Fugacious Air

Perk
Name Description
What are the Lives of the Meek, But Fugacious Air Greatly raises AGL for 3 turns and removes some status ailments when the user's HP drops to 70% or less
If the user has not acted this turn, then the user will be the next unit to act, regardless of original turn order
(Perk activates 1 time per battle)
This perk can be triggered when the attack is from an ally
